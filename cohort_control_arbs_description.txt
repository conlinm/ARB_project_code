Control for ARBs Study Cohort
ARBs Study Cohort
Tags
Prod_Group: Conlin
Cohort Entry Events
People with continuous observation of 365 days before event may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

drug eras of 'Non_ARB_antihypertensive', with era length > 60 days.
Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

Inclusion Criteria
1. Age > 18
Entry events with the following event criteria: who are > 18 years old.

2. No prior RCCa
Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'renal cell carcinoma'.

3. No familial/genetic risk of Rcca
Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'GeneticHereditaryRCCa'.

4. No ESRD
Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'ESRD_ARBstudy', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date.

5. No Transplant
Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Transplant_status_ARBstudy', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date.

6. No ARB use
Entry events having no drug exposures of 'ARBs'.

Cohort Exit
The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation. The person exits the cohort when encountering any of the following events:

condition occurrences of 'renal cell carcinoma'.
death of any form.
Cohort Eras
Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
